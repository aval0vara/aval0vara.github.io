Elena Carbajal is a curator and researcher focused on the intersections of contemporary art, emerging technologies, and cultural theory.

She works at Onkaos, where she supports artistic practices that critically engage with the cultural, aesthetic, and cognitive transformations shaped by technological artifacts and infrastructural systems.
